Personas

Subject 1

A low vision individual who uses a cane to help navigate around obstacles. She has the ability to see bright lights and blob-like objects. She can also rely on auditory processing skills to help her navigate.

Goals

  • To be autonomous in her everyday life.
  • To have the ability to explore new areas while feeling comfortable.
  • To not feel like she needs to make the easy choice when ordering a meal.

Observations

Navigating Towards An Intersection
  • Relies on her cane to avoid all obstacles on the floor.
  • Uses her acoustic surrounding much less then the totally blind person when walking towards the intersection.
  • Must rely on the intersection being busy with people as she crosses it while using the “following a crowd” method.
  • Has the ability to walk very fast (faster than all the observers).
Navigating Through a Building for the Restrooms
  • Relies heavily on instructions provided to her by someone else for navigation.
  • Relies on lighting conditions to generally tell her position.
  • Uses her cane to avoid smaller obstacles.
  • Is capable of locating the correct restroom without the need to read the sign (?).
Finding the Counter & Ordering Lunch
  • Is capable of avoiding chairs and tables with the use of her cane and her low vision.
  • Relies on the person behind the counter to help her choose a meal. However, chooses the easiest option so as to not burden the helper.
